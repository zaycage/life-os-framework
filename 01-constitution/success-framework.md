# Success Framework

## Principles

How I choose to live.

Examples include:

- Discipline
- Ownership
- Integrity
- Courage
- Patience
- Curiosity
- Humility

## Levers

What I can intentionally improve.

- Systems
- Friction
- Energy
- Direction
- Surface area
- Preparedness

## Outcomes

What naturally emerges.

- Consistency
- Compounding
- Luck
- Success

## Success Equation

\[
Success =
\frac{(Systems \times Consistency \times Time)^{Direction} \times Luck}
{Friction}
\]

Where:

\[
Luck = 1 + (SurfaceArea \times Preparedness)
\]

The equation describes the mechanics of success. The three-layer framework explains how a person operates those mechanics.
